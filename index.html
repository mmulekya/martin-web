KAKUMA REFUGEE CAMP SINCE 1992 TO 2025.

KAKUMA REFUGEE CAMP

kakuma refugee camp was established in the year 1992 by the diplaced communities from south sudan due to heavy war in the country , the main reciption was Loki , meanwhile the kenyan government decided to settle the displaced communities kakuma as the main refugee center. Today kakuma is not only madeof south sudanese but many nationalities from the neigboring countries those that face war, hunger and persecution from the country of origin.

SERVICES GIVEN TO REFUGEES.

kakuma is found by UNHCR to help refugees in the following of the programs such as shelter, Education and the basic needs such as water, food and accomondation please click here to learn more about UNHCR

CULTURE PRACTICES IN KAKUMA.

Today kakuma has turned to be the most place where the following activities, such as sportscultural dance, commercial centers, vocational trainings take place and more other activities that take place in the camp
